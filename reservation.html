Reservation Policy

For any questions about our reservation policy, please call us at (555) 123-4567.

Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the reserved time. Please call us if you're running late.
For parties of 5 or more, a credit card may be required to secure your reservation. This helps us manage our limited seating effectively.
24-hour notice is required for cancellations to avoid a cancellation fee. For large parties (5+), cancellations within 12 hours may incur a fee of $25 per person.
Dress code: Business casual. No beachwear or athletic attire please. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone not adhering to our dress code.
For birthdays, anniversaries, or other celebrations, please let us know in advance. We can arrange for special desserts or decorations (additional charges may apply).
